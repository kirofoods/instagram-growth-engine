Privacy Policy

KiroGram - Instagram Growth Engine by Shinsekai Enterprises Pvt Ltd (Kiro Foods)

1. Information We Collect

KiroGram collects the following data when you use our application:

  • Instagram Profile Data: Public profile information including username, follower count, following count, post count, biography, profile picture URL, and engagement metrics. This data is fetched via the Instagram Graph API and/or third-party services (Apify) with your explicit consent.
  • Account Settings: Your app preferences, notification settings, and theme preferences stored in Firebase Firestore.
  • API Tokens: API keys you provide (Instagram Access Token, Apify API Token) are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Ad Campaign Data: Campaign plans, budgets, and performance metrics you create within the app, stored in Firebase Firestore.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data exclusively to:

  • Display your Instagram analytics and growth metrics within the KiroGram dashboard
  • Provide personalized content recommendations and growth strategies
  • Track your progress toward follower milestones (0 to 1M roadmap)
  • Manage and optimize your Instagram ad campaigns via the Meta Marketing API
  • Sync your data across devices via Firebase Firestore
  • Generate competitor analysis reports and trend insights

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore (asia-south1 region, Mumbai). API tokens are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage and are never sent to any server other than the respective API provider (Meta/Instagram, Apify). All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Data is only transmitted to:

  • Meta/Instagram API: To fetch your profile data and manage ad campaigns (with your authorization)
  • Apify: To scrape publicly available Instagram data for trend analysis (using your API token)
  • Google Firebase: For secure data storage and cross-device synchronization

5. User Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time through KiroGram Settings → Data Management → Clear All Data. This permanently removes all stored data from Firebase Firestore. To delete locally stored API tokens, clear your browser's localStorage. You may also request complete data deletion by emailing us at shinsekaienterprisespvtltd@gmail.com.

6. Meta Platform Data

KiroGram accesses Meta Platform data (Instagram and Facebook) through the official Meta Graph API and Marketing API. We comply with Meta's Platform Terms and Developer Policies. We do not store Meta user access tokens on our servers — they are stored only in the user's browser. We use the minimum necessary permissions to provide our services.

7. Children's Privacy

KiroGram is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
